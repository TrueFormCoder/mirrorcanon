# MirrorCanon™ Red-Flag Token List (v1.1)

**Status:** Active  
**Purpose:** Trigger mandatory review when drift-risk language appears

Presence of any red-flag token requires justification or removal.  
If aesthetic rather than structural, remove it.

---

## A. Pronouns (Canon)

- you
- we
- our
- us

---

## B. Hedging and Softening

- maybe
- arguably
- it seems
- perhaps
- possibly
- sort of
- kind of
- often feels

---

## C. Therapy / Pop-Psych Lexicon (Banned)

- trauma
- healing
- narcissist / narcissism
- toxic
- abuse
- gaslighting
- closure (as feeling)
- boundary (as self-help)

---

## D. Fandom / Narrative Lexicon (Banned)

- arc
- journey
- redemption
- character development
- protagonist (as person)
- villain

---

## E. Advice / Motivation Language (Banned)

- should
- need to
- must (unless formal rule)
- best practice
- takeaway
- lesson

---

## F. Vibe / Impression Language (Banned)

- feels like
- vibes
- energy
- in my opinion

---

## G. Emotional Adjectives (Review Required)

- cruel
- cold
- manipulative
- abusive
- benevolent

If used, define as functional conditions, not moral labels.

---

## Resolution Rule

Ask: is this word doing structural work or aesthetic work?

- Structural: define formally or replace with operational language.
- Aesthetic: remove.
